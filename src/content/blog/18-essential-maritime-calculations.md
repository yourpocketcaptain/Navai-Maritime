### 8. Ship Stability & Loading
Stability is the difference between a safe voyage and a disaster. Calculate your Metacentric Height ($GM$) and stay within IMO limits.

### 9. Dynamic Squat Estimator
As you enter shallow water, your ship 'sinks' and 'trims'. Predict this effect based on your speed and block coefficient ($C_b$).

$$\delta_{max} = \frac{C_b \times V^2}{100}$$
