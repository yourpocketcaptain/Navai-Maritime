# Smart Watches for Sailors: Quatix 7 vs. Apple Watch Ultra

For the gadget-loving sailor, the wrist is the final frontier. 

## Garmin Quatix 7

This is a specialized maritime tool. It connects directly to your Garmin chartplotter. You can steer the autopilot, mark waypoints, and view wind data right on your wrist.
*   **Battery:** Weeks, not days.
*   **Screen:** Transflective (perfect in sunlight, dull inside).

## Apple Watch Ultra 2

The best smartwatch, period. But is it the best for *sailing*?
With apps like Navionics and Orca, it's getting there. The screen is essentially a mini-lighthouse (3000 nits), visible in the brightest conditions.
*   **Battery:** 2-3 days max.
*   **Ecosystem:** Infinite apps.
